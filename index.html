About Breezyscapes

“I started with a simple lawn care service right here in North Port, where I graduated. Over the years, that small operation grew into a full-service landscaping and irrigation company — and as the work evolved, so did the look. That’s how Breezyscapes was born. Since then, my team and I have proudly served the North Port area, watching it grow from just a Walmart into the vibrant, family-focused community it is today — with places like Wellen Park leading the way. Today, with a passionate team behind me, Breezyscapes combines technical expertise with a creative edge. We love bringing your outdoor vision to life — and my team is here to help guide and shape that vision every step of the way.”
